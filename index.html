18/04/2026 Congratulations to Matt Robinson who was announced as the Supporters Player of the Season for the 2025/26 season. An almost ever-present during the season, Matt controlled games brilliantly and scored key winners including against Maidstone, Horsham and Tonbridge. The Horsham strike also won Goal of the Season.
