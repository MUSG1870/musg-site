Privacy Policy

Maidenhead United Supporters Group (MUSG) is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy.

We process personal data in accordance with applicable UK data protection laws.

Who We Are

Maidenhead United Supporters Group (MUSG) is the data controller for any personal information collected through this website.

What Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including your name and email address when you:

  • Sign up to our newsletter
  • Complete membership or contact forms
  • Register interest in events or travel
  • Submit entries for Player of the Season awards

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide updates about MUSG activities
  • Organise travel and events
  • Communicate important supporter information

Email Communications

We use Mailchimp to manage our mailing list. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in our emails.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except where required to deliver services (e.g. email distribution platforms).

Data Retention

We only keep your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services or until you request its removal.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data, but no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You may also unsubscribe from communications at any time.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us via the website contact page.
